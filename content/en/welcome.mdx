## About This Platform

This is an intellectual platform dedicated to deep writing in Arabic. Our goal is to create an exceptional reading experience worthy of serious Arabic content.

We believe reading quality begins with precise details: font size, line spacing, column width, and the right typeface selection.

## Why Does Design Matter?

Arabic readers deserve a reading experience no less refined than what exists in other languages. Too many Arabic websites treat text as an afterthought rather than the primary content.

Here, the text comes first. No ornamentation, no distraction — just the words.

## Numbers and Technology

This platform was built in 2026 using the latest technologies: Next.js 16 and Tailwind CSS v4. The Arabic typeface is **Markazi Text**, designed specifically for comfortable Arabic reading. English articles use **Times New Roman** — a classical serif that carries intellectual weight.
